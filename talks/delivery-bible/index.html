People and Process

Responsibility for the Value

Everyone is responsible for value their works brings to the company and a product.

Responsibility for the value (2)

If something keeping you from doing valuable work, it's your responsibility to solve a problem.

If you don't know a value of what you do - it's your reponsibility to find it out or stop doing what doesn't bring value.

You could and would be asked about value of your work.

The Main Virtue

The main quality we seek in our coworkers is ability to get things done.

  • Finishing what you've started.
  • Doing what you've promised to do.
  • Knowing a balance between perfectionism and time to get things done.

Prioritizing

For everybody to be able to do valuable work, we need to have clear priorities in every one of our activities. And we need people responsible for establishing these priorities.

Definition of done

To be able to do right things we need to have definition of done for every project and task.

Decisions and authority

For every decisions that needs to be made there should be one person who makes final decision.

Ideally, everyone should know who it is for every possible decision.
